Your meetings,

Cueflow watches your Gmail for meeting invitations, then automatically joins and records the meeting with ffmpeg — even when you're away from your computer. Control everything from Telegram.

Everything you need

Set it once. It handles the rest.

📧

Email-triggered

Watches your Gmail inbox over IMAP and detects meeting invitations automatically. No OAuth — just a Google App Password.

📅

Calendar-aware

Parses ICS attachments and recurring events to schedule recordings days in advance, so nothing slips through.

🎥

ffmpeg recording

Bundled ffmpeg captures your screen with system audio. Multi-monitor selection, native resolution, MKV→MP4.

⚡

Flows

Independent, named automation pipelines with subject/sender filters. Run many in parallel, enable or pause each one.

💬

Telegram control

Rich notifications and inline buttons. Join meetings, stop recordings, and get the file — all from your phone.

🔒

100% local & private

No cloud, no account, no server. Credentials are encrypted on your machine. Your recordings never leave your PC.

How it works

From email to recording, automatically

1

Connect Gmail

Paste your address and a Google App Password. Optionally connect a Telegram bot for remote control.

2

Create a Flow

Set a subject filter (e.g. your course name) and the meeting type. Cueflow starts watching your inbox.

3

Walk away

When a matching meeting arrives, Cueflow joins it, records it, saves the file, and pings you on Telegram.
